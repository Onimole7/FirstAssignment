Basic Homemade Pancakes

A simple, fluffy, and delicious pancake recipe perfect for breakfast. This recipe is easy to follow and requires basic ingredients you already have at home.

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 25 minutes
  • Servings: 6
  • Difficulty: Easy

Finished Dish

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter

Instructions

  1. Mix all dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Whisk milk, egg, and butter in a separate bowl.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients until just mixed.
  4. Heat a skillet over medium heat and grease lightly.
  5. Pour batter and cook until golden on both sides.
